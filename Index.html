About

SC Sandore

Shekeyla (SC) Sandore is the Principal Advisor and Founder of Iron Arbor Advisory. Prior to founding the firm, she was a Consultant at Boston Consulting Group, where she led engagements in digital transformation, operating model design, and executive performance reporting.

Before BCG, she spent five years at Wellington Management Company, holding roles in global multi-asset strategy and institutional consultant relations, with primary coverage of over $25 billion in assets.

SC's work centers on helping boards, executive leaders, and business owners navigate complex strategic decisions — translating rigorous analysis into decisions leaders can stand behind.

From the Founder

Smart organizations often fall into predictable patterns of failure — not from lack of effort, but from lack of well-built internal systems and infrastructure.

Iron Arbor Advisory exists to solve that problem.

I've been a key player in well-funded global corporations and in boardrooms of much smaller, budget-conscious, mission-driven organizations. The same pattern appeared across them all: the gap between a successfully implemented strategy and a good idea that never leaves the off-site almost always comes down to internal infrastructure — the frameworks, systems, and clarity that lets leaders lead instead of getting trapped in a cycle of daily fire drills.

Iron Arbor Advisory is built on that premise. I work with boards, executive leaders, and business owners navigating complex strategic decisions — whether that's a growing local business, a nonprofit, or an organization in transition.

The work looks different depending on who you are. The need is usually the same.

— SC Sandore, Principal Advisor & Founder
